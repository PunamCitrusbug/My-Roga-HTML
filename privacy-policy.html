Cookies and Web Beacons

“Cookies” are unique identifiers that we transfer to your device to enable our systems to recognize your device. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used on our Services or in our emails. Both Cookies and Web Beacons (also known as pixel tags) help us analyze our web page flow; customize our services, content and advertising; measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety.

We automatically collect information using Cookies and Web Beacons. We use them to see which areas and features are popular, analyze our campaigns effectiveness, check email read rate and to count visits to our Services. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although doing so may interfere with your use of some of our sites or services.
